# Whale Tracking - Smart Money Analysis

## What is Whale Tracking?

Monitoring large wallet movements to identify:

- Early accumulation (smart money buying)
- Distribution (smart money selling)
- Insider activity (dev wallets, team wallets)
- Market manipulation (coordinated pumps/dumps)

## Remember

Whales are smart, but they're not always right:

- They can be wrong (even whales lose money)
- They can manipulate (pump their bags, then dump on you)
- They have different timeframes (they might hold through 50% dumps)

**Use whale tracking as ONE data point, not the whole story.**
